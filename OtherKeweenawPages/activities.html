Camp Keweenaw

Activities

Whether you're traveling north of the Houghton Bridge to hike to the top of Mount Baldy, to sample the many fine beers of local breweries or to relax and be in awe of the natural beauty, you'll find all that and more. The Keweenaw Yoopers know how to enjoy the outdoors. One of the most popular places to visit up here is Copper Harbor at the tippy top of the Keweenaw. To list a few things to do and see while here:mountain biking, hiking, local artistry shops, breweries and restaurants in Copper Harbor, Houghton, Hancock, Calumet and other great towns in the area. Oh, and the Calument Theater, mining history/tours and Swedetown recreation area, Houghton Tech...
