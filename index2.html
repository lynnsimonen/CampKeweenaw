Visit Our Family Cabin on Lake Superior

Camp Keweenaw (pronounced KEE-wa-naw) is a rustic A-frame cottage located about 40 feet away from Lake Superior in Gay, Michigan. Depending on the year (and the whims of Lake Superior) we may have a nice sandy beach or a sandstone beach. The beach is walkable from the back door up to the lovely Tobacco River park. The Tobacco River is about 300 yards away and is great for steelhead (rainbow trout) fishing, if that is your passion. We have a campfire pit, sauna, and beautiful sounds of waves at night. We are within a mile of ATV trails and the infamous Gay Bar. We are also about 5 miles away from a protected marina in Big Travers.
